A) What is reverse-i-search in a Linux terminal?
Ans: In a Linux terminal, the reverse-i-search (reverse incremental search) is a feature that allows you to quickly search through your command history for previously entered commands that match a specific pattern. This is a convenient way to recall and reuse commands without manually scrolling through your command history.
